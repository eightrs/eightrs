# Eight Rs — Public Concept (Safe Release)

Eight Rs introduces “Reward” as the eighth R of recycling.

The platform issues a Stellar-based digital asset called **8RS** to
incentivize verified recycling actions. This allows low-value or zero-value
recyclable materials to gain measurable reward.

## Summary

Eight Rs connects:

- consumers
- recycling centers
- local businesses
- sustainability organizations

By rewarding small positive actions, Eight Rs increases recycling rates and
supports environmental goals using fast, low-energy blockchain technology.
